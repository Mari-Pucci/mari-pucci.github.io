Rules specific to the Fallen Claws

  1. Loyalty to Fallen Claws comes before anything else. You are expected to put your life on the line for the sake of the group. Betrayal of any kind will be dealt with accordingly.
  2. Cross clan friendships are allowed. If facing friends in battle, you are expected to put Fallen Claws first. Failure to do so, will result in punishment.
  3. Cross clan relationships will result in punishment if uncovered.
  4. The words of the higher ranks are final and they expect respect and obedience.
  5. Only healers should have access to herbs and poisons.
  6. Only Elites and Higher Ranks should have reinforced claws.
  7. If prey shortages occur, Healers, Queens, Elders and Kits are fed first.
  8. All Fallen Claws members (besides Leader and Healers) are expected to sleep within Fallen Claws’ camp or surrounding buildings.
  9. Warriors are allowed up to two learners at a time.
  10. Kits are not allowed to leave camp.
  11. Learners are not allowed to leave camp without a trusted adult.
  12. Failure to comply to Fallen Claws rulings will see punishments.
  13. All members are expected to take a rogue name. NO clan / tribe names. Those who joined during Shiversight’s reign are free to keep their name, any members joining after are expected to change.
  14. Once joining Fallen Claws, the group becomes your new family. You are expected to treat them as such, and fight for them as if they are your blood.

    15. These rules aren't here to trip you up, instead they should provide fun opportunities for RPs!
